Finally have sorted out the firmware for the DCC Coloured Light Signal Decoder and made the dedicated pcb's.

These decoders respond to the following aspects:

```
Aspect   Output
0        Red
1        Green
2        Amber
3        Amber Amber
4        Flashing Red
5        Flashing Green
6        Flashing Amber
7        Alternate Flash
8        Alternate Fade Flash
9        Double Strobe
10       Single Strobe
31       Dark
```

The first three are the same as RED/GREEN/AMBER in exrail.  All aspects can be sent via ASPECT command in EX-Rail or <A .. ..> from the command station.

They are available to suit common anode or common cathode led light signals.  Either will work with incandescent globes upto 100mA.

Operating voltage at the LEDs can be 5V from track power or 3-24V from external power supply.

LEDs will require a current limiting resistor.

I'm looking at adding other aspects to the above list.

The decoder can be setup via CV to be 8 2-aspect, 5 3-aspect or 4 4-aspect signals.
